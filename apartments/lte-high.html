About This Property

Living the HIGH experience! This unique apartment is filled with positive energy. Open space design with high ceilings, new and innovative features throughout. Relax and fill your energy in our aerial yoga hammock.

The apartment is on the 1st floor of a house, very warm and cosy with automated heating and ventilation. Enjoy our unique bathroom with wood details and floor heating, comfortable sleeping room with high-quality mattress, and a big open well-equipped kitchen. Self check-in available, close to city center with bus stops just 20m away.
